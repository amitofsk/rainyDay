The Motions of Water in Angola, Indiana

This is a Visualization Tool for seeing the changing Lakes & Rivers of Angola, Indiana

Description

This is a site that does an analysis over a small history of Angola's water on it's lakes and rivers. We made this information page about the local hydrology near where we live. Using information from multiple satellites that can found in the references section at the bottom of the page. We focused on Angola Indiana, near GPS coordinates 41.650137, -85.017476. This page includes monthly rain data. It also variations in water over a year period. We also included a timelapse of shortwave radiation over the North-East U.S.A.


This page was made by Conner Upton with the combined help of Conner Kauffmann and Andrea Mitofsky for the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge.
